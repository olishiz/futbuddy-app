About Us
What is FutBuddy?
FutBuddy is a better way to organise and play football with friends. We bring you payment solutions, scheduling tools, reminders and more. Organise and attend your favourite trainings, connect with friends and meet new people - all with our 1-click solution.
How do I book a game?

To book a game:

1. Navigate to your chosen game.

2. Tap “Book Slot”.

3. Choose your method of payment: Stripe. Note that all payments will be processed in MYR.

4. Follow the instructions from our payment provider to complete the purchase.

5. Upon successful payment, your first name will appear on the players list and you will receive a confirmation email with your booking details as well as a receipt of your purchase. Please allow a couple of minutes for the emails to arrive.

6. Show up and have fun!

Cancellation Policy
Please make sure to cancel your spot at least 24 hours before the game starts. If you cancel within the 24 hour period, you are not entitled to a refund. To cancel, please send a WhatsApp message to Oliver (+60168694900) stating that you would like to cancel your booking slot.
